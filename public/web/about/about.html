About PRISM

Empowering Filipino consumers through transparency

We're on a mission to democratize price information in the Philippines

PRISM was born from a simple observation: Filipino consumers and businesses lack access to reliable, real-time price data. This information asymmetry leads to unfair pricing, market inefficiencies, and millions of pesos in overpayments every year.

Our AI-powered platform aggregates data from thousands of sources - from palengkes to supermarkets, DTI SRP to online stores - and turns it into actionable intelligence for everyone.

Our Mission

To eliminate price opacity in the Philippine market by providing accessible, accurate, and real-time price intelligence that empowers consumers to make informed decisions and helps businesses compete fairly.

Our Vision

A Philippines where every consumer knows the fair value of every product, where businesses thrive through transparency, and where market inefficiencies are eliminated through data-driven insights.

Our Core Values

Transparency

We believe in open, accessible price information. No hidden fees, no obscure data - just clear, honest pricing intelligence you can trust. Our goal is to empower you with transparent insights that make decision-making simple.

Protection

We protect consumers from unfair pricing and businesses from market manipulation. Fairness is at the core of everything we do, guiding our standards to build a more transparent and balanced marketplace for everyone.

Integrity

Our AI models are built to be unbiased, and our recommendations are carefully designed to uphold market fairness. Every insight we deliver is grounded in accuracy to serve the best interests of consumers.

Intelligence

We continuously improve our AI, expand our data sources, enhance analytics capabilities, and develop innovative, cutting-edge tools to make price intelligence smarter, faster, and more actionable than ever before.

Our Journey

The Idea


Conceived at a hackathon focused on consumer protection, PRISM addresses the lack of price transparency in the Philippines, helping ensure fair economic practices.

Early Development


Beta version launched with basic price checking and DTI SRP comparison. Collecting initial feedback from early users

AI Integration


Machine learning models for anomaly detection and fairness scoring to ensure prices are accurate and equitable.

Vision
