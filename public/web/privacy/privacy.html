1. Overview

At PRISM, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform, website, and services (collectively, the "Services").

Our Commitment: We believe in transparency. We only collect data necessary to provide accurate price intelligence and improve your experience. We never sell your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and password when you register.
  • Profile Information: Profile photo, location preferences, and user type (consumer/supplier).
  • Payment Information: Billing details (processed securely by third-party payment processors).
  • Communications: Messages sent through our platform, customer support inquiries.

2.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent, search queries.
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers.
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address for local price comparisons.
  • Price Data: Products you search for, prices you view, and price alerts you set.

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • DTI SRP data and PSA inflation statistics for price comparison.
  • Publicly available price information from partner suppliers.
  • Social media information if you choose to connect your account.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide Services: Process your searches, display price comparisons, connect you with suppliers.
  • Improve AI Models: Train our algorithms to detect price anomalies and predict trends.
  • Personalization: Show relevant products and price alerts based on your preferences.
  • Communication: Send service updates, price alerts, and respond to inquiries.
  • Security: Detect fraudulent activity, unauthorized access, and enforce our terms.
  • Analytics: Understand usage patterns to improve platform performance.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data in these limited circumstances:

  • With Suppliers: When you initiate contact through Marketplace, relevant information is shared to facilitate transactions.
  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who help us operate (hosting, analytics, payment processing).
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or to protect rights and safety.
  • Aggregated Data: Anonymized, non-identifiable data shared with partners like DTI for market insights.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of marketing communications.
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format.

To exercise your rights, contact us at prism@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256).
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing.
  • Access controls and multi-factor authentication for employees.
  • Secure data centers with 24/7 monitoring.

Note: While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission is 100% secure. You also play a role by keeping your account credentials safe.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide Services. After account deletion, we may retain anonymized data for analytics and legal compliance (typically 30-90 days for backups).

8. Children's Privacy

PRISM is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we discover we have collected information from a child, we will delete it immediately.

9. Local Data Transfers

PRISM is based in the Philippines. All personal data is collected, processed, and stored within the Philippines. We do not transfer your data to other countries.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via email or platform notice. Your continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
